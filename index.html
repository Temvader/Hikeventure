About us

Hikeventure was born from a passion for the unexplored.

Founded in 2015 by a group of mountaineers and explorers, our company started with a simple idea: to show the world adventures beyond mainstream tourism. The first routes were literally mapped out "on the go" — guides led clients along trails absent from guidebooks, slept in tents under the stars, and cooked meals over campfires.
Over the years, Hikeventure evolved into a community of like-minded adventurers, where every journey is not just a trip but a personal challenge. We kept the pioneer spirit alive but paired it with flawless execution, safety, and comfort.
Today, we operate expeditions in 30+ countries, from Himalayan treks to ski-touring in the Alps. Yet we still believe: true adventure begins where the pavement ends.
